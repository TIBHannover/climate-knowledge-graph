3.2 Report quantification - version 1.0 manual count

  • A scoping literature review shows no comprehensive AR6 or IPCC Assement Reports quantification.
  • We believe what has been collected is the first attempt at such measurement.
  • An automated count will be carried out using TIB Grobi software https://projects.tib.eu/grobi in Q4 2025.
  • Data sources and data collection will take place in a follow up report.

3.4 What is the AR6 report

AR6 is comprised of seven reports:

  • the Synthesis Report (referred to as SYR)
  • the 3 Working group reports (referred to as WGI, WGII, WGIII)
  • the 3 Special reports (referred to as SR1.5, SRCCL, SROCC)
